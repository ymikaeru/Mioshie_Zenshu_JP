ON CANCER

Eikoh, No.158, May 28, 1952 (the 27th year of Showa)

　　In the preceding issue, our fellow believer Bunji Tatematsu staying in the U.S. reported that the disease that American people fear the most is cancer, so I will write about the source of this disease. The causal agent of cancer is very simple; it is toxins accumulated by meat-eating. You also should understand this fact by observing that those who are most likely to suffer from this disease are not the young but the middle-aged and older. Therefore, in order to be immune to the cancer you should eat a lot of vegetables. As I have written before, it is the best for a human being to have equal quantities of meat and vegetables. The reason is that meat is Yang as food, while vegetable is Yin [yang and yin is the metaphysical concept of polarity in Chinese traditional philosophy; yin-yang represents the dual qualities complementary rather than mutually exclusive, so they cannot exist without each other, e.g. passive-active, shade-light, winter-summer, water-fire, tender-tough, feminine-masculine, plant-animal, etc.]; accordingly, to eat either of them excessively and disproportionately is go against the laws of nature. Therefore, if you should contract cancer, you must switch to a vegetable diet as soon as possible, and you will surely be cured even though it might take a long time.

　　However, I give you a warning here: if, having contracted this disease, you take medicines or receive radium treatment, it is all the worse likely that you will never be cured, so I cannot answer for such a case by any means. Therefore, if you do not have any medical treatment, you will sure be cured and need not worry at all.

　　In addition, let me write about other diseases prevailing in America. Besides cancer, people have been suffering from polio for some time, but this is false polio and the true cause of the case is that injection drugs and other medicines get hard in legs. In order to cure this condition, there are no other remedies than Johrei, but for now, since it is impossible to practice Johrei in this country, as the second best, when they first leave it to the process of nature, it will be cured by natural purification though gradually, so they have no choice but to do this way. However, not knowing this, they complicate it by undergoing medical treatment, so that it becomes an incurable disease for life. And the next to be mentioned is tuberculosis. With regard to this disease, I will soon publish "Faith Cure for Tuberculosis" and distribute it also in the U.S., so whoever reads this book would be saved. Fortunately, since the mentality of the American people is progressive not sticking stubbornly to conventional theories, they will be able to recognize my account earlier than expected.



癌病に就て

『栄光』158号、昭和27(1952)年5月28日発行

　前号に在米国の信者立松文二君よりの通信中に、同国人が現在最も怖れている病気は癌の病だというので、この病原についてかいてみるが、これははなはだ簡単な原因で、肉食の毒素が溜るのである。この病気が若人になく、中年以後に発（おこ）るにみても分るであろう。従ってこの病気を免れるには、野菜を多く食えばいい。以前もかいた事があるが、人間は肉と野菜と半々くらいがちょうどいいのである。この理由はどういう訳かというと肉食は陽性食物であり、菜食は陰性食物であるから、偏ると天理に外れる事になる。従って万一癌病に罹ったとしたら、直ちに菜食に切換えれば長くはかかるが必ず治るのである。

　しかしここに注意すべき事は、罹病するや薬を用いたり、ラジウムの療法などをすると、反って治らなくなるから請合えない。ゆえに何らの療法もしなければ治るから心配はないのである。

　ついでに他の病気の事もかいてみるが、同国においては癌の外小児麻痺にも以前から困っているが、これは擬似小児麻痺で、原因は注射その他の薬毒が足に固まるからであって、これを治すには浄霊より外はないが、今のところ同国では不可能だから、まず自然に放置しておけば、自然浄化によって僅かずつでも治るので、それより外に仕方がない。だがそれを知らないため、医療によって拗（こじ）らしてしまい、一生涯不治の病となるのである。次は結核であるが、これは今度結核信仰療法を出版し、同国へも頒布（はんぷ）するから、これを読んだら救われるが、都合のいい事には米国人は進歩的だから、古い説に余り拘泥（こうでい）しない以上、案外早く認識さるるであろう。
